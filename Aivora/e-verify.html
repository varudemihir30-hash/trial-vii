E-Verify Participation

Vertex Tech IO is an E-Verify participating organization in the United States. We are committed to lawful, transparent, and ethical employment practices and follow U.S. work authorization verification standards as required.

E-Verify is a U.S. governmentauthorized system that enables employers to confirm an individuals eligibility to work in the United States. As an E-Verified organization, Vertex Tech IO supports hiring processes that align with E-Verify requirements and promotes compliant employment pathways for candidates and employers.

While final employment verification is conducted by the hiring employer, our internal processes are designed to maintain compliance, transparency, and accountability throughout the staffing and placement lifecycle.
